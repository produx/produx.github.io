About The Jewel facing Rockefeller Center

Modern Rockefeller Center hotel with a private club

Next to Rockefeller Plaza

In Midtown Manhattan, The Jewel facing Rockefeller Center places you right next to Rockefeller Center Promenade and Radio City Music Hall. World-famous shopping thoroughfare Fifth Avenue lines one end of the block, while green oasis Central Park is a 10-minute stroll north.
