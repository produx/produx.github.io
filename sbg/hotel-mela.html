About Hotel Mela Times Square

Luxury hotel with a fitness center in Midtown Manhattan

6 minutes to Bryant Park

The Hotel Mela Times Square is on West 44th Street, a 2-minute walk from Times Square's entertainment and dining. The Times Square/42nd St. Subway Station is a 4-minute walk, and the Southwest Porch lounge and carousel at Bryant Park are 6 minutes away on foot.
