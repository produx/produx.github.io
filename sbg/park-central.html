About Park Central Hotel New York

Prime Midtown Manhattan location, 3 blocks from Central Park

Short walk to Broadway

Park Central New York offers a great Midtown location 1 block from Carnegie Hall and 3 blocks south of Central Park. Rockefeller Center's Top of the Rock observation deck is 9 minutes on foot; Times Square and dozens of Broadway theaters are within a 10-minute walk.
