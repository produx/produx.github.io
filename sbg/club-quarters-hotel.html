About Club Quarters Hotel, Grand Central

Modern, friendly hotel next to Grand Central Station

Times Square and trains

The Club Quarters Grand Central is located in midtown Manhattan right next to Grand Central Station, making further exploration easy. The must-see attractions of Times Square and Fifth Avenue are minutes away.
